Buffalo chicken lettuce wraps

Description

Super easy dinner recipe that comes straight from Tik Tok.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Ranch seasoning
  • Buffalo hot sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Blue cheese

Steps

  1. Put the chicken breasts, ranch seasoning, and hot sauce in a slow cooker
  2. Serve the shredded chicken in lettuce leaves and sprinkle with crumbled blue cheese and a little more hot sauce
